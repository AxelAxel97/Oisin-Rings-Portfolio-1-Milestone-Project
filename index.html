Our Story

Growing up in the Sussex countryside, I spent countless days exploring the chalk hills and sprawling meadows. Nurturing an early appreciation for nature’s rugged beauty. This connection to my surroundings influences every piece I create, as I draw inspiration from the Sussex landscapes, local architecture, and ancient artistry. Each piece is handcrafted with a focus on form and natural texture, combining sterling silver with vibrant gemstones like ruby, sapphire, and emerald. The result is jewellery that feels both raw and refined - evocative of treasures unearthed from an archaeological site. I also offer commissions for engagement rings, wedding bands, or any bespoke projects you have in mind. Get in touch and let's create something meaningful and uniquely yours.
